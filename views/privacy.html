Information that is gathered from visitors

In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details such as the visitor's IP address, browser type, referring page and time of visit. Cookies may be used to remember that you're logged in Where registration is required, a username will be stored on the server, along with a hashed password and a randomly generated color

How the Information is used

The information is used to enhance the vistor's experience when using the website to display personalised content We do not collect E-mail addresses.

Visitor Options

You may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website, such as logging in.

Cookies

Cookies are small digital signature files that are stored by your web browser that allow your preferences to be recorded when visiting the website. Also they may be used to track your return visits to the website.
